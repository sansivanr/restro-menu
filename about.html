ABOUT

Welcome to Silver Goose, where hard work, passion, and uncompromising quality have paved the way to our undeniable success. We believe that good food speaks volumes, especially when paired with a warm and inviting atmosphere and friendly faces.

When you step into Silver Goose, we want you to taste the essence of homemade goodness, the dedication we pour into each sandwich, and the joy we strive to infuse into every bite. Our team, including myself, is committed to creating an experience that leaves a lasting memory with you long after you've left our doors.

We invite you to join us on Silver Goose, where the fusion of history, passion, and mouthwatering flavors awaits. Experience the magic that has made High Street Market and Deli a cherished destination for locals and visitors alike.

~ Saikat
